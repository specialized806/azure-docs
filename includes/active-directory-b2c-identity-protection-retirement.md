> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Azure AD External Identities P2 retirement in B2C tenants:** On March 15, 2026, P2 and ID Protection will be retired in B2C tenants. Existing P2 customers will continue to be charged until they switch to P1. To continue protecting against identity-based risks, migrate to a [partner identity verification provider](../articles/active-directory-b2c/identity-verification-proofing.md). To switch to Azure AD External Identities P1, [change your pricing tier](../articles/active-directory-b2c/billing.md#change-your-azure-ad-pricing-tier). For questions, contact your Microsoft Account Manager or [contact support](../articles/active-directory-b2c/find-help-open-support-ticket.md).
